Google Shopping List Setup

Follow these steps to connect your Google Shopping List to SuiteMeal

Important Security Information

The Google Master Token is equivalent to your Google account password. It provides full access to your Google account. Guard it carefully and never share it with anyone.

Only enter your Master Token in applications you completely trust. Treat it with the same level of security as your Google password.

Your Privacy Matters

SuiteMeal does not store your Google Master Token on any server. Your token is stored only on your device and is used exclusively to sync with your Google Shopping List.

The token never leaves your device except to authenticate directly with Google's services.

Method 1: Using Browser Security Settings

  1. Login: Navigate to https://accounts.google.com/EmbeddedSetup, sign in with your Google account, and accept the terms
  2. Access Security: Find and click the lock icon (or site information icon) in your browser's address bar
  3. Open Cookies: Select the "Cookies" or "Site Settings" section from the security menu
  4. Expand Domain: Look for and expand the accounts.google.com dropdown
  5. Copy Token: Locate the cookie named oauth_token and copy its value from the Content field
  6. Use in App: Paste this token into SuiteMeal when prompted for your Google Shopping List credentials

Method 2: Using Browser Developer Tools

  1. Login: Navigate to https://accounts.google.com/EmbeddedSetup, sign in with your Google account, and accept the terms
  2. Open Developer Tools: Press F12 (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Option+I (Mac) to launch Developer Tools
  3. Navigate to Storage: Click on the "Application" tab (Chrome/Edge) or "Storage" tab (Firefox)
  4. Find Cookies: In the left sidebar, expand the "Cookies" dropdown menu, then click on https://accounts.google.com, find the oauth_token cookie in the list, and copy its value
  5. Use in App: Paste this token into SuiteMeal when prompted for your Google Shopping List credentials

What Happens Next

The oauth_token cookie you copy will be an Authentication Token (format: oauth2_4/...). Simply paste this token into SuiteMeal, and the app will automatically convert it to a Master Token for ongoing access to your Google Shopping List.
