Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 7, 2026

SuiteMeal ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the SuiteMeal mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

SuiteMeal is designed with your privacy in mind. By default (free tier), all data you create — including recipes, meal plans, and shopping lists — is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

If you upgrade to SuiteMeal Pro, your recipes, meal plans, and shopping lists are stored on our secure servers solely to enable cross-device sync. This data is associated with your Pro account and is not sold or shared with third parties.

Regardless of plan, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information beyond what is required for a Pro account (e.g. email address)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or analytics data
  • Usage or behavioral data

Google Integration

If you choose to connect SuiteMeal to your Google Shopping List, you will provide a Google authentication token. This token is stored exclusively on your device and is used solely to communicate with Google's services on your behalf. It is never transmitted to SuiteMeal's servers or shared with any third party.

Third-Party Services

SuiteMeal does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Any third-party services you connect (such as Google) are governed by their respective privacy policies.

Data Storage and Security

For free users, all app data is stored locally on your device using the security mechanisms provided by your operating system. We have no access to this data. If you delete the App, all locally stored data will be removed.

For Pro users, data is additionally stored on our servers to enable cross-device sync. We use industry-standard security practices to protect this data. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

SuiteMeal is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at customer.support@suitemeal.com.
